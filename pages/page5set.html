Myki Rate & Concession


As International students, you are eligible to apply for Myki concession card which entitles you to a 50% discounted rate when travelling with public transport.
To apply, click here

• Zone 1+2: travel in all suburbs of the Melbourne metropolitan network on trains, trams and buses. $799.50 due at time of purchase

• Zone 2: travel in the outer suburbs of the Melbourne metropolitan network on trains, buses and some trams. $546.00 due at time of purchase
